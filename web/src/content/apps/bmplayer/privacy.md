When you create an account, we collect:

| Data | Purpose |

The App displays advertisements provided by **Google AdMob** (and potentially Google's advertising partners). AdMob and its partners may collect and use information to show and measure ads, which can include:

- Device identifiers (such as advertising IDs on mobile)  
- IP address  
- General device and app usage data related to ad delivery and performance  

How Google uses data is described in Google's policies, including:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)  
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)  

You can limit ad personalization on many devices via your device settings (for example, "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android). That does not always mean you will see no ads—only that ads may be less tailored.
